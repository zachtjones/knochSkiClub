FYI

After School Trips (Monday-Friday):
Depart from the high school gym after
school. Equipment may be delivered
before school (7:00 am - 7:30 am) to
the KHS gym or after school to the bus.
Do not deliver equipment to a school
office of during the school day.
Weekend/Day trips will leave and return
to the high school parking lot unless
otherwise directed.

Trip Cancellations:
If a trip is cancelled, pick up your check
in H326 (Mr.Brumbaugh). These
payments will not be credited to
future trips. You may reuse the check for
another trip, but it is your responsibility to
attach it to another flyer.

Guests: As a KDA member, you may bring
a guest with you. You and your guest must
meet with me in person at least a week
before the trip. You must be willing to be
responsible for your guest?s behavior. A
member may only take two guests per year,
and any certain guest may only participate in
two KDA trips per year. (No guests may be
permitted on the overnight trip unless the trip
has available space after the deadline.) On
trips that fill to capacity, guests may be asked
to cancel to make room for members.

Equipment: Likewise, if you are just starting
or trying the sport, you are not expected to
spend a couple hundred dollars on equipment
for one snow sport event. Rental is available
either at the resort or from one of our local
shops (See about page).

Parents/Chaperones: Parents are welcome
to be a part of KDA activities and are needed
to keep trips safe and efficient. As participants,
parents will be expected to demonstrate moral
and ethical character in their words and actions.
Parents should not use tobacco or alcohol
before or during any activity. For the activity
in which you would like to participate, write a
note of intent on the member's trip flyer. Cost
for parents is the same as members. Parents
do NOT need to complete the GUEST form.

Due Dates for Registration: Follow
directions on each form. Deliver all forms
to the KDA Box in either the high school
or middle school office. Because of the
amount of planning and scheduling required
for each trip, the number of participants
must be determined in advance. Trips will
be planned based on the number of forms
submitted on time. If any space is available,
late registrations will be accepted on a first
come/first serve basis. (You must deliver
late registrations directly to Mr. Brumbaugh.)

All contacts are on the contact us page
