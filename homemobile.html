KDA, (Knights and Days of Adventure),
was originally started as Knoch Ski Club in
1987 by a student, Scott Gumto. Through
the years, it has grown and diversified to
include other adventurous activities such as
rafting, camping, caving, and rock climbing.
In 2004 KDA was adopted as an outreach
ministry of Jefferson Center Presbyterian
Church. In 2010, KDA became its own
non-profit organization with the same goals.

Membership is open to anyone grades 7-12
that lives in the South Butler County School
district.

Parents are always welcome and
encouraged to participate in KDA
activites.

Over the years, many people (bus drivers,
group sales representatives, parents, and
other skiers/boarders) have complimented
KDA members for being polite, courteous,
responsible, and respectful. KDA members
have accepted the challenge to have fun
without the use of drugs, including alcohol,
or tobacco, and treat other people as
children of God.
